How to Play Loop Runner

Learn the controls, scoring system, modes, and strategies to consistently hit the leaderboard.

Controls

  • Move: Mouse cursor guides your runner.
  • Fire: Right-click to shoot. You have 3 rounds that auto-recharge.
  • Pause: P or Esc.
  • Restart: Spacebar.

Scoring & Combos

Each enemy destroyed adds to your score. Defeating enemies quickly chains a combo that multiplies points. Keep your chain alive by staying aggressive and accurate.

Power-ups

  • Shield: Absorbs one hit so you can keep your combo.
  • Rapid: Temporarily increases fire rate.
  • Magnet: Pulls pickups and boosts your sustain.

Game Modes

  • Normal: Endless survival with progressive difficulty.
  • Daily Run: Same pattern for everyone on a given day. Scores reset daily at 00:00 UTC.

Performance Tips

  • Use a mouse (desktop) for best precision.
  • Close background tabs to reduce stutter.
  • Toggle your browser’s hardware acceleration if you notice frame drops.
