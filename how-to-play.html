How to Play Loop Runner

Controls, tips, power‑ups, and scoring strategy for Loop Runner — the fast browser reflex game.

Loop Runner is easy to learn and hard to master. Your runner follows the mouse cursor. Survive as long as possible, rack up combos, and climb the global leaderboard.

Controls

  • Move: move your mouse — the runner follows.
  • Fire: right‑click to unleash a radial burst. You have 3 rounds that auto‑recharge over time.
  • Pause: press P or Esc. Press Space to start a new run.

Power‑ups

  • Recharge (⚡): instantly restores your fire rounds.
  • Multiplier (x3): triples your current score — cash in smartly after a strong chain.

Scoring & strategy

  1. Keep your combo alive. Chaining eliminations increases the points you earn per hit.
  2. Use bursts reactively. Don’t spam — wait for clusters to maximize impact and safety.
  3. Control space. Herd enemies to the screen edge, then slip through gaps.
  4. Daily runs. The daily seed means every player gets the same enemy pattern on a given day. Practice, analyze, and improve.

FAQ

Is Loop Runner free? Yes. It’s 100% free to play in your browser.

Do I need an account? No. You can enter a display name for leaderboards, but no signup is required.

Does it work on mobile? Yes, but a mouse or trackpad offers the highest precision.
