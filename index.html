Loop Runner — Free Reflex Browser Game

Loop Runner is a free, fast-paced browser game built by Playloop. Dash through endless waves, chain combos, and climb the leaderboard. No downloads — just pure skill and reflex action directly in your browser. Try the Daily Run where everyone faces the same pattern each day.

Tip: Right-click or tap to fire (3 rounds, auto-recharge). Drag to move on touch. Space to restart, P/Esc to pause.

What's in Loop Runner

  • Eight rotating themes. The whole cast of enemies redraws every three minutes — Jurassic dinosaurs, cyberpunk drones, deep-sea anglerfish, underworld demons, mythical knights, cosmic comets, glitch corruption, and steampunk clockwork. Survive the full 24-minute revolution to unlock the "circled all themes" reward banner.
  • Boss waves. At score milestones (5,000 / 15,000 / 30,000) a single tougher enemy spawns: 2.4× the normal size, with a visible HP bar, drawn in whatever theme is currently active.
  • Daily Run. A shared daily seed — every player faces the same enemy patterns, pickups, and timing. Compete head-to-head on the daily leaderboard and check yesterday's podium in the Hall of Fame.
  • Roguelite upgrades. Crossing score thresholds auto-grants a random upgrade from a pool: Ring Burst, Piercing Rounds, Quick Reload, Heavy Caliber, Time Warp, Powerup Magnet, and more.
  • Worldwide and country leaderboards. Submit your name and country once; rankings update live. Switch the leaderboard scope to your country to chase a local podium.
  • Plays anywhere. Mouse + right-click on desktop, touch + tap on mobile, on-screen pause for both.

How to play

Move the mouse to steer your ship — it follows the cursor. Right-click to fire (3 rounds, auto-recharging). Chain kills to build a combo multiplier, dodge what you can't shoot, and keep the chain alive for exponential score. Full control reference and tips on the How to Play page.

Frequently asked

Is Loop Runner free?

Yes — fully free, no downloads, no accounts. A leaderboard submission only asks for a display name.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Drag with one finger to move; tap with a second finger to fire. The on-screen pause button is in the bottom-left.

What's a "Daily Run"?

A shared seed regenerates each day. Every Daily Run player faces identical enemies in the same order at the same speed, so the leaderboard rewards skill rather than luck. Yesterday's top 3 are archived in the Hall of Fame.

Why are there bosses and themes?

To keep long runs interesting. The devlog covers the full design rationale.
