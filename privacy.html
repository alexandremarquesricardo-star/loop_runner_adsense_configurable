Privacy Policy

We keep things simple: minimal data, clear choices, and no surprise tracking.

Overview

Playloop operates the website located at playloop.run. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What We Collect

  • Runtime data: basic event data needed to run the game (e.g., score submissions, mode selection, date).
  • Ad services: Google AdSense may process device and contextual information to serve ads.

Cookies & Consent

We implement Google Consent Mode with a default of “denied.” Ad and analytics storage is only enabled after you interact with the consent prompt. You can revisit choices via your browser’s site settings.

Scoreboard & Names

If you enter a display name for leaderboards, it will be shown publicly with your score. Avoid sharing personal information in your name.

Your Rights

For access or deletion requests, contact privacy@playloop.run. We’ll respond within 30 days.
