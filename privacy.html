Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for Playloop and Loop Runner — how ads and the leaderboard handle data.

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what limited data we handle and why.

At a glance

  • Loop Runner is free to play. We don’t sell personal data.
  • We use Google AdSense to show ads and Supabase to store leaderboard scores.
  • You can play without creating an account.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense. Google may use cookies or device identifiers to serve and measure ads. Where required, we display a consent message and respect your choices. Learn more about how Google uses information at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Leaderboard

Submitting a score sends a display name (you choose it), score, mode (e.g., daily), and a coarse country code derived from your IP (e.g., “GB”). We do not request or store email addresses for this feature.

Analytics

We currently do not use Google Analytics. If this changes, we’ll update this page and request consent where required.

Data retention

Leaderboard entries may be retained for the lifetime of the game. You can request removal of a specific entry by contacting us with your display name, score, and date.

Cookies

Ads may set cookies to function. You can control cookie behavior via your browser settings and the consent prompt.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the Playloop team at: hello@playloop.run
