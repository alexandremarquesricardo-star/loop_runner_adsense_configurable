About Playloop & Loop Runner

Learn what Playloop is, how Loop Runner was built, and what’s coming next.

Playloop is a tiny browser‑game lab based around a simple idea: delightful arcade loops you can play instantly — no downloads, no paywalls, no clutter. Our first release, Loop Runner, is a minimalist arena where your cursor control and reaction time are everything.

What makes Loop Runner different?

  • Instant play: loads fast and runs on any modern browser.
  • Pure skill: no gacha, no grind — just you versus the board.
  • Daily challenge: a seeded run so every player faces the same pattern each day.
  • Privacy‑respecting: we keep things lightweight and transparent.

Tech & craft

Loop Runner is hand‑coded with HTML5 canvas and modern JavaScript. We obsess over input latency, visual clarity, and feedback that feels crisp — from velocity‑tinted enemies to satisfying particle bursts. Leaderboards are powered by Supabase with row‑level security enabled for safe, anonymous submissions.

Roadmap

  • More power‑up variety and balanced difficulty ramps
  • Optional accessibility tweaks (reduced effects, larger UI)
  • Seasonal themed dailies and friendly competitions
