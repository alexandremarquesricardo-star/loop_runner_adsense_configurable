About Playloop

Playloop builds small, fast, and fair browser games. Loop Runner is our reflex-first arcade experiment: instant play, crisp controls, and transparent leaderboards.

Our Principles

We believe games should respect your time and your device. That’s why Loop Runner loads quickly, runs in any modern browser, and keeps inputs minimal yet expressive. We avoid dark patterns, obtrusive ads, and unfair mechanics.

Our roadmap focuses on performance, clarity, and community: clearer difficulty ramps, meaningful power-ups, and competitive modes like the Daily Run that put skill first.
